Every time I want to write a blogpost, I will have tremendous pressure. I don't want to write something that make others think I am stupid. I want to write something that is logical and thoughtful like Wait But Why. But then, with all these things in my head, I will never really write a post. So, I decided, from now on, I will just write whatever that is in my head. No one will give a shit to what I write. In fact, no one reads my blog at all.

So fuck it. This blog should be my little playground, my own kingdom. Just treat it as a notebook, a diary. I just want to write as freely as I felt in my first blog on Baidu when I was in secondary school and high school.
